Frequently asked questions.

Yes — natively. Marks out of 20, sequences and terms, coefficient-weighted averages, conduct and class council decisions, Ministry-format report cards, GCE mock results (A–U), and O-Level & A-Level transcripts are all built into the core. It works in both English and French.
